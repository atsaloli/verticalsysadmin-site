Training Program to Make a Novice System Administrator

May 5, 2016 · 1 min · Aleksey Tsalolikhin

A self-paced curriculum for preparing entry-level professionals for Linux sysadmin roles.

Part I – Linux Essentials#

  • Text: “Linux Essentials” (2015)
  • Online: Introduction to Linux (LFS101x) via edX
  • Investment: 40–60 hours, ~$26

Part II – The Linux Command Line#

  • Free PDF, 537 pages

Part III – Linux System Administration#

  • Linux Foundation’s Essentials course (LFS201), includes DNS training
  • 40–50 hours, $499 (certification included)

Part IV – Certification (Optional)#

  • LPIC-1 or Linux Foundation LFCS

Part V – Computer Science Fundamentals#

  • Harvard’s CS50x via edX (can begin after employment)

Total cost: ~$1,030. Time: 2–4 months full-time or 6–12 months part-time. Prerequisites: Basic computer literacy.
