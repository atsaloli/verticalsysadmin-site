Infrastructure Management at Scale

July 26, 2016 · 1 min · Aleksey Tsalolikhin

Presented at the Digital Media Educators Conference (DMEC) for California community colleges. Three core philosophical foundations:

  1. Promise Theory — voluntary cooperation and distributed work over direct control
  2. Pull vs. Push Architecture — pull-based systems’ advantages for configuration management
  3. Dunbar Numbers — applying relationship limits to constrain infrastructure information presentation. “The design of CFEngine uses Dunbar numbers to focus the information it presents.”

CFEngine Enterprise dashboard features: health indicators, alert management (color-coded), compliance tracking, progressive drill-down. Inventory and compliance reporting: custom inventory collection, multi-column sortable tables, pie charts, bar graphs, chart export for management reporting.
