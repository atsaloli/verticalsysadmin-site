## What is GitLab?

[GitLab](https://www.gitlab.com/) is a complete DevOps platform built around [Git](https://git-scm.com/) version control. It provides [issue tracking](https://about.gitlab.com/2016/08/22/announcing-the-gitlab-issue-board/), [continuous integration](https://about.gitlab.com/gitlab-ci/), and [much more](https://about.gitlab.com/features/#compare).
