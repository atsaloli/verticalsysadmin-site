Aleksey Tsalolikhin is the CEO and Principal Architect of Vertical Sysadmin, Inc.

He founded Vertical Sysadmin to deliver effective training in excellent technologies to operations practitioners. In 2011, the League of Professional System Administrators recognized him with their "Mentor of the Year" award, and he serves on their Board of Directors.
