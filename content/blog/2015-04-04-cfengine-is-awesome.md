References an [Enigmabox blog post (agent42, March 2014)](https://enigmabox.net/en/blog/2014/03/migrating-from-puppet-to-cfengine/) where the author found "CFEngine is 10 times faster, uses less memory" than alternatives, and praised the vendor's responsive support.

The author selected CFEngine for its longevity (since 1993) and broad system compatibility. When the author reported a performance bug, CFEngine's creator responded promptly and accelerated the next version's release to get the fix into production.

Community acknowledgment: [Neil Watson](http://evolvethinking.com/author/nwatson/) and [Ted Zlatanov](https://github.com/tzz) recognized for contributions and mentorship of new users. "CFEngine's technical strengths are matched by its community's character."
