---
title: "LOPSA Mentorship Program Protégé Ionut Cadariu Earns RHCE"
date: 2015-10-17
author: "Aleksey Tsalolikhin"
---

Aleksey shared a letter from mentee Ionut Cadariu (based in Romania), who wrote to express gratitude after achieving his goal of becoming a Red Hat Certified Engineer through the [LOPSA Mentorship Program](http://www.lopsa.org/mentor):

> "I finally achieved my long term objective -> to be Red Hat Certified Engineer…it was a long journey and I wanted to thank you for all the hard work you did with me in order to achieve this."

Aleksey described the letter as making him "ridiculously happy." Cadariu consistently completed work, responded promptly, and maintained an appreciative attitude. Aleksey congratulated him and encouraged him to pursue CCNA next, recommending physical networking equipment practice and [GNS3](http://www.gns3.com/) simulation software in the interim.
