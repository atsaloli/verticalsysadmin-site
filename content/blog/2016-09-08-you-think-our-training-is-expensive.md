Vertical Sysadmin charges $3,000/day plus a $2,000 admin fee for on-site training (up to 12 participants). Countering pricing objections by quoting Red Adair: "If you think it's expensive to hire a professional to do the job, wait until you hire an amateur."

## Training Methodology

- Active monitoring of student comprehension
- Modular progression — don't advance until all participants understand current material
- Theory-practice balance with hands-on labs after each section
- Foundational rigor before advancing to intermediate/sophisticated topics

No attendees reportedly complained about pricing after completing the program.
