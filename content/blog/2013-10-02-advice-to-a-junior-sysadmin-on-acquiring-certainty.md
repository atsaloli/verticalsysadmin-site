For a junior sysadmin who has practical experience but feels uncertain about knowledge gaps: use the Linux Professional Institute's LPIC-1 certification framework as a structured self-assessment.

## Three-Step Method

1. Print the [LPIC-1 exam requirements](http://www.lpi.org/linux-certifications/programs/lpic-1/exam-101) page
2. Checkmark topics already understood confidently
3. Study remaining unchecked items systematically, gaining hands-on experience until achieving competency before moving forward

Purpose: use the certification curriculum as a self-directed learning roadmap, not necessarily to get certified. Knowledge should be validated through actual hands-on work.
