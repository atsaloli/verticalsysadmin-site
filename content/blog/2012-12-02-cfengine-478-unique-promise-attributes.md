---
title: "CFEngine claims to be comprehensive. There are 478 unique promise attributes in CFEngine 3.4.0."
date: 2012-12-02
author: "Aleksey Tsalolikhin"
---

CFEngine 3.4.0 has 478 unique promise attributes enabling administrators to model nearly any aspect of system configuration. The platform addresses knowledge management challenges through features that "allow you to summarize and abstract the underlying low-level detail."
