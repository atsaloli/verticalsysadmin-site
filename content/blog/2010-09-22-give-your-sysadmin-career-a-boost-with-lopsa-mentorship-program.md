LOPSA (League of Professional System Administrators) mentorship initiative — volunteer-driven, connecting experienced sysadmins with aspiring professionals.

Mentors help "define the project goals, then let them work independently," acting as coaches. Projects should have clear endpoints (e.g., setting up specific infrastructure or services).

Proteges describe their desired projects in one paragraph, get matched with mentors, and submit brief project summaries upon completion.

About LOPSA: independent nonprofit, Mount Laurel, NJ. Phone: 856-439-0500 or 800-285-2141. Mentorship can develop "professional relationships that can last a lifetime."
