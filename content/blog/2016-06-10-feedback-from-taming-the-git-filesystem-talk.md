Mike Weilgart planned to repeat his "Taming the Git Filesystem" presentation on June 23rd in Burbank, following its debut on June 2nd at the UUASC-LA/LOPSA-LA meetup.

## Attendee Feedback

- **Stephen Franklin** (Systems Engineer): "filled out some conceptual holes" in his understanding
- **Eric White** (Senior System Engineer): "approachable and assumed no prior knowledge"
- **George Wu** (VP of Engineering): addressed gaps in practical Git knowledge
- **Eric** (Community College Student): able to understand the explanations despite limited background
- **Jordan Schwartz** (Systems/Storage Engineer): "has something to offer for most git users" — "not a run of the mill 'how to use' git presentation"
