Presented at the [Digital Media Educators Conference (DMEC)](http://ict-dm.net/faculty-pd/dmec) for California community colleges. Three core philosophical foundations:

1. **[Promise Theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Promise_theory)** — voluntary cooperation and distributed work over direct control
2. **Pull vs. Push Architecture** — pull-based systems' advantages for configuration management
3. **[Dunbar Numbers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dunbar%27s_number)** — applying relationship limits to constrain infrastructure information presentation. "The design of CFEngine uses Dunbar numbers to focus the information it presents."

CFEngine Enterprise dashboard features: health indicators, alert management (color-coded), compliance tracking, progressive drill-down. Inventory and compliance reporting: custom inventory collection, multi-column sortable tables, pie charts, bar graphs, chart export for management reporting.
